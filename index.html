These projections are estimates, not guarantees. The default 7.5% return is meant as a long-term planning assumption for broad funds that track the overall market, such as index funds. Individual company stocks can rise or fall for company-specific reasons, so their future growth cannot be predicted with the same simple compound-interest model.
